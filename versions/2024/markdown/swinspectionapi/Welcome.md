# Welcome

The SOLIDWORKS Inspection API:

- Helps you automate ballooned inspection drawings, quality inspection reports, and quality sampling sheets for SOLIDWORKS drawings.
- Allows you to save inspection characteristic data in SOLIDWORKS files, make changes, export to multiple inspection reports, and export ballooned documents to other formats such as PDF and eDrawings.
